ElectronGPT by KRW CLASSIC

This app is a simple app made with Electron that provides a standalone window for ChatGPT and includes some preloaded extensions. Preloaded extensions include:

  • "ChatGPT Coding Kit" - Made by me and not released anywhere else extension that adds a "Copy Code" button at the bottom of the code block.
  • "ChatGPT Optimizer 1.9.2" - Made by Productivity Rocks and modified/decompiled by Me to ensure it works in the Electron environment.

ElectronGPT is not affiliated with OpenAI or ChatGPT and is not their official product.
